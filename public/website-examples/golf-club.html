Bushveld fairways for memorable rounds.

Marula Fairways threads through koppies, fever trees, and roaming wildlife. Each hole balances championship challenge with Northern Limpopo calm — sunrise drives, gin wagons at dusk, and star-lit boma dinners after the 18th.

Experience the Waterberg in curated calm

Morning tee times launch from misty fairways, afternoons wind down at the spa, and evenings settle around crackling fires. Our field hosts and PGA pros choreograph each stay for families, corporate retreats, and serious golfers alike.

Signature field hospitality

24-hour lodge concierges, sunset gin tastings, and wildlife trackers who guide guests between villas, spa rituals, and tee boxes.

Golf performance studio

TrackMan fittings, short game laboratory, and elite coaching clinics run by Sunshine Tour professionals.

Safari & culinary pairings

Dawn game drives followed by chef-led brunch, evening nine-hole loops paired with boma tasting menus and live storytelling.

Wellness pavilion

Outdoor hydrotherapy pools, marula oil massages, and quiet meditation decks nestled under ancient mopane trees.
