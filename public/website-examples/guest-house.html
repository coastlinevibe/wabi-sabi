From arrival drum beats and rooibos welcome infusions to sunset boma tastings, Tamboti Ridge choreographs every moment to feel effortless, grounded, and distinctly Limpopo.

Marula fizz on arrival, oshibori towels, and a personalised scent ceremony anchored in veld botanicals.

Concierge-led adventures

Guided game drives, baobab art tours, and heli transfers to private reserves and cliff-top picnics.
