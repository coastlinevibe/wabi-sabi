Flame-fired food for bushveld nights.

Bushveld Range brings the bushveld to town: smoked steaks, potjie pies, bunny chow sliders, and charred greens served with heritage sides. Sip small-batch brews, rooibos sours, or chilled chenin while local acts set the vibe.

Events rooted in bushveld culture

Weekly happenings keep Bushveld Range buzzing — from sport screenings to sunrise markets and conservation fundraisers. We’re the local hangout with national flavour.

Friday Flame Sessions

Local DJs and braai specials under festoon lights with limited-edition tap pours.

Big Match Lounge

Wall-sized screens, supporter platters, and post-game karaoke for rugby and football nights.

Sunday Bush Market

Family brunch, kids fire pit s'mores, and pop-up stalls from Limpopo artisans.

Conservation Evenings

Guest talks from park rangers, themed tasting menus, and donations to veld projects.
