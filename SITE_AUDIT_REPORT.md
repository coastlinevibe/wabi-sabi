# Wabi-Sabi Website Audit Report
**Date**: May 9, 2026  
**Scope**: Content relevance, pricing consistency, messaging alignment

---

## 🎯 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Critical Finding: PRICING CONFLICT DETECTED ⚠️

The new **Support Desk** service (R1,000-R2,500/month) creates a **pricing perception issue** with the core consulting services (R45k-R180k). This needs strategic positioning to avoid:
- Devaluing premium consulting services
- Customer confusion about service tiers
- Cannibalization of high-value engagements

### Overall Assessment: **GOOD with Strategic Gaps**
- ✅ Core messaging is consistent and strong
- ✅ Wabi-Sabi philosophy well-articulated
- ⚠️ Pricing structure needs clarification
- ⚠️ Service positioning needs refinement
- ⚠️ Some outdated references need updating

---

## 📊 DETAILED FINDINGS

### 1. PRICING STRUCTURE ANALYSIS

#### Current Pricing Tiers:

**A. Core Consulting Services** (Work With Us page)
- **Discovery Session**: R5,000 (credited toward any plan)
- **Foundation**: R45,000 (30 days, 3 core shifts/apps)
- **Growth**: R95,000 (60 days, 6 shifts/apps) - Most Popular
- **Scale**: R180,000 (90 days, 12+ shifts/apps)
- **Optional Retainers**: 
  - Foundation: R7,500 base + 5-10% profit uplift
  - Growth: R15,000 base + 5-10% profit uplift
  - Scale: R25,000 base + 5-10% profit uplift

**B. Support Desk Services** (NEW - Support Desk page)
- **Starter Support**: R1,000/month (2 hours/month)
- **Growth Support**: R2,500/month (5 hours/month)
- **Custom Build**: Quoted

**C. Training Programs** (Training page)
- **AI Essentials**: From R12k (1 day or 3×2h)
- **Growth in Operations**: From R35k (2 days)
- **Leadership Development**: From R25k (half/full day)

#### 🚨 PRICING CONFLICT ISSUES:

1. **Name Collision**: "Growth" appears in both consulting (R95k) and support desk (R2.5k/month)
2. **Value Perception**: R1k/month support could make R45k consulting seem overpriced
3. **Customer Journey Confusion**: Unclear when to choose support vs. consulting
4. **Positioning Gap**: No clear explanation of how services relate to each other

## 🎯 STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### PRIORITY 1: RESOLVE PRICING CONFLICT (CRITICAL)

#### Option A: Position Support Desk as "Entry Point" ✅ RECOMMENDED
**Strategy**: Support Desk is for ongoing maintenance; Consulting is for transformation

**Implementation**:
1. Add positioning statement to Support Desk page:
   ```
   "Already have systems in place? Our Support Desk keeps them running smoothly.
   Need to build or transform your systems? Explore our Consulting Services."
   ```

2. Add CTA on Support Desk page:
   ```
   "Need more than support? Book a Discovery Session to explore our 
   transformation consulting (from R45k)"
   ```

3. Add CTA on Work With Us page:
   ```
   "Not ready for a full transformation? Start with our Support Desk 
   (from R1k/month) for ongoing digital support."
   ```

4. Rename Support Desk "Growth Support" to "Priority Support" to avoid name collision

#### Option B: Separate Target Audiences
**Strategy**: Support Desk for <R500k revenue; Consulting for R500k+ revenue

**Implementation**:
1. Add revenue qualifier to Support Desk page
2. Make it clear these are different service lines
3. Update homepage to show both paths

#### Option C: Make Support Desk Post-Consulting Only
**Strategy**: Support Desk is only available after completing a consulting engagement

**Implementation**:
1. Remove Support Desk from public navigation
2. Offer it as "ongoing support" after project completion
3. Position as retention/upsell

**RECOMMENDATION**: **Option A** - It's the most flexible and doesn't limit market reach

## 🎬 CONCLUSION

**Overall Site Health**: **8/10**

The Wabi-Sabi website is strong, with excellent messaging, clear value proposition, and compelling case studies. The main issue is the **pricing and positioning conflict** introduced by the new Support Desk service.

**Key Takeaway**: The Support Desk is a good addition, but it needs clear positioning to avoid confusing or devaluing the core consulting services. With the recommended changes, it can serve as an effective entry point or ongoing support option without cannibalizing high-value engagements.
