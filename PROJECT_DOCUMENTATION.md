# Wabi-Sabi Project Documentation

## Project Overview

**Wabi-Sabi** is a business growth consultancy platform that helps companies discover hidden profit opportunities through systems optimization, automation, and strategic clarity. The philosophy centers on finding beauty in imperfection—working with what businesses already have rather than forcing complete overhauls.

**Core Value Proposition:** Reveal hidden value in existing business systems, shape growth wisely through small strategic shifts, and build lasting profit through clarity and discipline.

**Target Audience:** Business owners and leaders generating R500k+ annual revenue who feel stretched thin despite success, seeking sustainable growth without adding complexity.

## Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Amber/Orange gradient (`#FF8A29` to `#FFB347` to `#FF7A1C`)
- **Accent:** Green for growth (`#22c55e`, `#16a34a`)
- **Background:** Slate dark tones (`#0f172a`, `#1e293b`, `#334155`)
- **Text:** White and slate variations for hierarchy

### Typography
- **Headings:** Sora font family, extrabold weights
- **Body:** Inter font family, light to medium weights
- **Tracking:** Wide letter-spacing on labels (0.28em-0.32em)

### Button Styles
- **Primary:** Orange gradient with white text, 18px radius, shadow lift on hover
- **Secondary:** Transparent with border, white text, subtle fill on hover
- **Padding:** `px-6 py-3` for comfortable touch targets

### Card Styles
- **Border Radius:** 14px for deliberate, structured feel
- **Borders:** Subtle rgba borders with gradient washes
- **Backdrop:** Blur effects on overlays
- **Shadows:** Vertical lift shadows for depth
